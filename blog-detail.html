When do we use saunas?

A sauna session is wonderfully relaxing and soothing. That's why you want to enjoy it as often as possible. But how many times a week is that allowed? And what are the benefits of regular sauna use? You read it here!
How often in the sauna is wise?
Visiting the sauna is healthy for your body. There is therefore no limit to the number of sauna sessions that you can do per week or per month. In fact, a study from the Journal Neurology found that taking a hot air bath four to seven times a week cuts your risk of having a stroke by 60%. Do you think, four to seven times a week, that is very often? That's because the research was conducted in Finland, where the sauna is a daily activity. But also in the Netherlands you can enjoy the health benefits of a sauna in your own sauna at home. It is important to know that you lose a lot of moisture with the high temperatures. So you can go to the sauna five times a week if you want, but make sure you drink enough.
How many sauna rounds at a time?
A sauna round lasts between 8 and 15 minutes. Depending on how often you use the sauna, you can adhere to the following rules of thumb. Do you go to the sauna once a week? Then count three sessions. Do you go to the sauna twice a week? Then two sessions are enough. Do you go three times a week or more? Then hold one session. The more often you go to the sauna, the fewer rounds are needed. These are, of course, guidelines. Always listen to your body. Do you feel that you can no longer tolerate the high temperatures? Then simply leave the sauna earlier or plan a session less.
What are the benefits of going to the sauna regularly?
If you go into an infrared cabin or other sauna several times a week, you will enjoy the health benefits the most. You build a better resistance, have less chance of cardiovascular disease, have a better condition and you will feel more positive mentally!
